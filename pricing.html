            Pricing

            Value That Scales—Pricing with Transparency & Strategy

            What affects the price?

            We don't believe in generic pricing. Financial operations vary significantly; some companies have straightforward needs, while others demand intricate solutions. Here’s a look at the factors that customize your monthly investment:

            • Monthly Transaction Volume.
            • Total Reconciled Financial Channels (Banks, Credit Cards, Loans).
            • Disbursement Complexity (Physical Checks vs. Digital Payments).
            • Number of Merchant/Gateway Platforms.
            • Annual Business Revenue.
            • Accounts Receivable Management (Matching Customer Payments).
            • Vendor Payables Service (Bill Payment Management).
            • Segmented Reporting Needs (Job, Location, or Class Tracking).
            • Advisory and Review Frequency.

            Pricing Steps

            Are you ready to strategically outsource your financial management? Finovix Solutions is dedicated to making this process seamless, efficient, and transparent for you. Clear communication, meticulous accuracy, and unwavering consistency are the cornerstones of our client relationships. Review our specialized services to confirm the package that aligns with your goals.

            01

            Discovery Meeting

            It starts with an initial consultation all about your business—understanding your unique needs, goals, and working style. Aligning our approach with your vision is the first critical step toward long-term success.

            02

            Diagnostic Review

            The next step is a paid Diagnostic Review to take a deeper look into the current state of your financials and operational needs. This essential service ranges between $199–$499 depending on the complexity and size of your business. This diagnosis delivers an accurate quote for fixing or maintaining your books, provides you with peace of mind, and lays out a clear, custom path for your business growth.

            03

            Strategic Pricing Session

            Next, we craft a custom financial proposal just for you. We review the comprehensive diagnostic findings and combine them with your long-term goals to create a personalized service proposal and pricing structure.

            04

            Commitment & Proposal

            Once the custom pricing is finalized, you'll receive a tailored proposal. This will be either for a one-time Financial Reset Project if your books require significant organization, OR a monthly service plan with a 12-month commitment if your books are clean or newly established.

            05

            Formal Onboarding

            The final onboarding session is dedicated to ensuring every detail in your proposal is meticulously finalized. By the end of this meeting, every promise made is captured and ready for execution—guaranteeing your transition to Finovix Solutions is smooth, complete, and effective from day one.
